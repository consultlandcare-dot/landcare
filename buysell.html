Title Verification

Before buying or selling a property, it is essential to verify its legal background. LandCare conducts thorough due diligence, including ownership history, land records, and encumbrance checks, to ensure the property is legally clear and safe for transaction.

On-Site Documentation Assistance

Property transactions often require physical verification and coordination at the site location. We provide on-site documentation support, helping clients with inspections, document collection, and interaction with local authorities when required.

Sale Deed / Gift / Exchange

Drafting and registering property documents requires precision and legal compliance. LandCare assists with sale deeds, gift deeds, and exchange deeds by ensuring all documentation is accurate, legally valid, and smoothly executed.

NRI Buy / Sell

NRIs often face challenges when managing property transactions remotely. We offer specialized assistance for NRI buy and sell cases, including situations where the pattadar is unavailable, ensuring a smooth and legally compliant process.
