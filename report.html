Report an Issue

LandCare aims to present property and land-related information responsibly. If you notice an inaccuracy, discrepancy, or concern, you may report it for review.

What Can Be Reported

  • Incorrect or outdated property information
  • Document-related inconsistencies
  • Boundary or location mismatches
  • Service-related concerns
  • Website content or technical issues

How LandCare Handles Reported Issues

Reported issues are reviewed internally to assess whether the information requires correction, clarification, or removal.

LandCare may rely on publicly available records, client-provided documents, or third-party sources while reviewing reported concerns.

What This Page Is Not

  • Not a legal complaint or dispute resolution platform
  • Not a substitute for legal or government verification
  • Not a guarantee of correction or outcome
Important:
LandCare does not make ownership determinations or legal judgments. Users are advised to consult qualified legal or government authorities for official verification or dispute resolution.
