Frequently Asked Questions

What is Passbook Correction?

It is the process of correcting errors in the land passbook details.

What is a survey number mismatch?

It occurs when the survey number in records does not match the actual land details.

What is extent correction?

It is the correction of the recorded land area to match the actual extent.

What is nature of land correction?

It is updating the land classification in official records.
